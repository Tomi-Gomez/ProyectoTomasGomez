About me

Well, as you have already seen on the home page, I am a “Front-end developer”. Now I want to talk about how I got to this point. My beginnings in iformatics lie approximately since I was 6 years old, when I had my first computer. I started with the games that came installed on the machine. As the years began to pass I began to install them on my own, that process as insignificant as it can be today to install a game, marked my life a lot. Since then I knew that technology was going to be my thing, from the installation of games to the installation of programs for business jobs and others. But I only knew the basics, after finishing high school, I asked myself what I wanted for my life, and the first thing that crossed my mind was something related to technology. I started looking for related careers, and I didn't like any of them, one day browsing Yotube, I found a well-known programmer "Soy Dalto" in which he explains and gives courses on how to program, at the moment I saw some lines of code and those lines were reflected on a web page, my head exploded. From that moment I knew it was my thing, and it was what I was looking for so much.

Since that day I started with Coder and I will continue with them to continue adding knowledge!
