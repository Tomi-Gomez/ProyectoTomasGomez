Spotify

Spotify is a digital music, podcast, and video service that gives you access to millions of songs and other content from creators around the world.
Basic features like listening to music are completely free, but you also have the option to upgrade to Spotify Premium.
Whether you have Premium or not, you can do the following:
Receive recommendations based on your tastes Create music collections and podcasts And much more...

Spotify is available on a wide variety of devices, including computers, phones, tablets, speakers, TVs and cars, and you can easily switch between them with Spotify Connect.

Can i save music from Spotify?

Spotify music and podcasts can only be accessed from within the apps. Our license means that there is no way to export our content outside of the app.

Snapchat

Snapchat is a messaging app for smartphones with multimedia support of image, video and filters for reality photos increased.

How does it work?

At its base, Snapchat works similar to an instant messaging app, allowing you to add contacts and send them messages in the form of photos or videos individually or to groups. The main difference is that you select how long the recipient can see that message (from 1 to 10 seconds) before it is destroyed.
That is, you send your photo, edit it if you want (you can add a small text or draw on it) and send it to whoever you want, specifying the time that they can access it. The user is then notified that they have a message and can view it by pressing on the message for the time specified by the sender (if they stop pressing, they stop viewing it).
